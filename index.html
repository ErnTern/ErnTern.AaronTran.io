Personal Project

This project compares tennis legend Roger Federer in 2006 with the top players of 2023.

My question for this research is "Would 2006 Federer be able to compete in today's ATP tour?" My findings indicate that Roger Federer was exceptionally dominant on the ATP Tour in 2006. His service game was unparalleled when compared to the top players of 2023. Additionally, Federer maintained a remarkably consistent ranking, averaging the number one spot, and achieved the highest number of match wins among all players. According to the charts if 2006 Roger Federer were to be placed in today's ATP tour, he would at minimum be a top 5 ranked player.
